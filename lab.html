How the Overnight Factory Works

A fully autonomous loop — no humans in the loop between sunset and sunrise.

Step 1 — 11:00 PM
🔍
Research
The agent scans developer communities, trending repos, and market gaps to identify high-demand skill opportunities.
Step 2 — 11:30 PM
⚙️
Generate
Each skill is built from scratch — SKILL.md manifest, implementation code, error handling, and documentation, all following OpenClaw standards.
Step 3 — 12:00 AM
🛡️
Scan
Every generated skill runs through the ClawForce Tier 1 scanner — 22 vulnerability patterns, secret detection, and risk scoring.
Step 4 — 12:30 AM
🚀
Deploy
Skills scoring below the risk threshold get automatically deployed to the skill registry. High-risk skills are flagged for review.
