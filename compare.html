The Honest Take

Dependency scanning is useful. World-class security research is valuable. Neither of those solves the problem of verifying the AI agent skills that are running on your machine right now.

If you're using Claude Code, Cursor, Copilot, or Windsurf, you're granting skills access to your filesystem, your terminal, and your network. ClawForce answers four questions those other tools don't:

  • What skills is your agent using?
  • Who published them?
  • What can they access?
  • Can you verify any of this?
