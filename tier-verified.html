The Identity Problem

Non-deterministic zip files break SHA-256 verification. The same skill packaged on macOS vs Linux produces different hashes due to compression timestamps, case sensitivity, and path separator differences.

ClawForce solves this with content normalization: we hash the actual code, not the archive. Your identity hash stays the same no matter who packages your skill.
