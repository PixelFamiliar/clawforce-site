Three Steps to Revenue

From zero to earning in under five minutes. No code changes to your website.

1️⃣
Install the Edge Router
Deploy our Cloudflare Worker in front of your domain. It sits between the internet and your website, screening every visitor.
  • One-click Cloudflare deployment
  • Works with any website or CMS
  • No changes to your existing code
  • Global edge network (~4ms latency)
2️⃣
Set Your Price
Open the Merchant Portal and set your rate per API request. $0.001 per hit, $0.05, $1.00. You decide what your data is worth.

  • Full transparency for both sides

Every verified AI agent that accesses your site pays your rate. Automatically. No invoicing, no contracts, no negotiations.
