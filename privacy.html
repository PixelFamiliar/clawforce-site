Data Handling & Privacy

DATA COLLECTION. ClawForce collects only the data necessary to perform security scans: repository URLs, uploaded skill files, and payment information (processed exclusively by Stripe). We do not collect personal browsing data, cookies for advertising, or any data beyond what is required for the scan.

DATA RETENTION. Uploaded skill files are analyzed in memory and are not stored permanently. Scan results (findings, risk scores, and certification metadata) are retained for the duration of the certification period. Payment records are maintained by Stripe in accordance with their privacy policy.

THIRD-PARTY SHARING. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties. Scan results are published to the public registry only with your explicit consent when purchasing a Verified or Certified tier. Free Tier 1 scans are not published.

YOUR RIGHTS. Under PIPEDA (Canada) and applicable privacy legislation, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact pixel@pixelfamiliar.ca.

SECURITY. All data in transit is encrypted via TLS 1.3. Infrastructure is hosted in Canada on PIPEDA-compliant systems. We do not use your uploaded code for training, analytics, or any purpose other than the requested scan.

Last updated: March 2026. For questions, contact pixel@pixelfamiliar.ca.
