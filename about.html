About ClawForce

ClawForce is the economic layer for the AI web. We sit between AI agents and the websites they access, enabling micro-payments per request so both sides benefit.

The problem

AI agents crawl the web at massive scale. They extract data, summarize content, and build products on top of it. The website owners who create that content? They get nothing. Most don't even know it's happening.

The result: website owners start blocking AI traffic entirely, and AI developers lose access to the data they need.

Our solution

ClawForce deploys an edge proxy (built on Cloudflare Workers) in front of any website. When an AI agent sends a request, our proxy verifies the agent's token, checks their prepaid balance, charges a micro-fee set by the merchant, and forwards the request.

Merchants earn revenue from every AI visit. Developers get guaranteed, authenticated access. Both sides win.

How it's built

The edge router runs on Cloudflare's global network, adding ~4ms of latency. Token verification uses SHA-256. Billing runs through Stripe. The merchant portal and developer dashboard are built on Next.js and Supabase.

The system is designed to be vendor-neutral: any website can join as a merchant, any AI agent can register as a developer. No lock-in.
