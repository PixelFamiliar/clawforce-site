Frequently Asked Questions

What is ClawForce?
ClawForce is the "Agent Tollbooth" for the web. It's an edge proxy that sits between AI agents and your website. When an AI agent visits, ClawForce verifies their token, charges a micro-fee you set, and forwards the request. You earn money from every AI visit.
How is this different from just blocking bots?
Blocking AI agents means you lose potential revenue and visibility. ClawForce lets you monetize that traffic instead. Verified agents pay to access your content; unverified bots are still blocked. Best of both worlds.
How much does it cost for merchants (website owners)?
Nothing. Installing ClawForce is free. You earn revenue from AI agents who pay to access your content. ClawForce takes a small platform fee from each transaction.
How much do developers pay?
Developers pay per request at rates set by each merchant, typically between $0.001 and $0.05 per request. You fund your wallet starting at $5 via Stripe. No subscriptions, no monthly fees.
Do I need to change my website code?
No. ClawForce deploys as a Cloudflare Worker in front of your existing domain. Your site runs exactly as before. The edge proxy only intercepts requests from identified AI agents.
How do you detect AI agents?
ClawForce uses token-based authentication. Registered AI agents include their ClawForce API token in the request headers. The edge router verifies the token via SHA-256 signature matching. Requests without valid tokens are handled according to your policy (block, allow, or challenge).
What if an AI agent doesn't have a token?
You configure the behavior. Options: block the request (returns 403), let it through for free (standard crawling), or return a challenge page that directs the agent to register with ClawForce.
How fast is it?
The edge router adds approximately 4ms of latency. It runs on Cloudflare's global edge network, so verification happens at the closest data center to the request origin.
Can I set different prices for different pages?
Currently, pricing is per-domain. Path-level pricing and tiered pricing based on data type are on the roadmap.
How do I get paid?
Revenue accumulates in your merchant account. Payouts are processed via Stripe to your bank account. You can view earnings in real-time through the Merchant Portal.
Is ClawForce open source?
The ClawForce protocol and token specification are open. The edge router implementation and platform are proprietary, run by Pixel Familiar Inc. The system is designed to be vendor-neutral with no lock-in.
